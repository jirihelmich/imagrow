ImaGrow

Free desktop application for monitoring the growth of prematurely born children — built on Czech reference auxological data and LMS percentile charts.

↓ Download (macOS & Windows) Read the user guide

What it does

Built for neonatologists and paediatricians who follow up children born before term.

Percentile growth charts

Weight, length, head circumference and weight-for-length plotted against 2nd / 5th / 50th / 95th / 98th percentile curves derived from LMS quantile regression.

Live preview while typing

As you enter weight, length and head circumference, four charts update in real time — you see where the value lands before you click save.

Local and private

Patient data stays on your computer. No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry. Backups happen via a one-click JSON export.

Export charts as PNG

One click to download any growth chart as a 2× resolution PNG with the patient's name, ready to paste into a discharge report or e-mail.

Built on Czech reference data

Reference percentile curves are derived from a longitudinal study at the Centre of Comprehensive Care, KDDL VFN Prague — 1,781 children and 5,676 examinations between 2001 and 2015.

Supported by the project “New methods in follow-up care for children with perinatal burden at CKP KDDL VFN” (reg. NF‑CZ11‑OV‑1‑009‑2015), funded by a grant from Norway through the EEA & Norway Grants programme.
